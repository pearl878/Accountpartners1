Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Accountpartners offer?

We offer bookkeeping, tax filing, auditing, payroll support, stocktaking and business consulting tailored to your needs.

Who do you work with?

We support startups, SMEs, and growing businesses across Nigeria looking for structure and financial clarity.

Do you offer custom service bundles?

Yes! We create flexible packages based on your business model, goals, and budget.

Can you help with tax compliance?

Absolutely. We prepare tax documents, advise on payment schedules, and support you through filing seasons.

Do you train staff on accounting tools?

Yes, we offer training to help your team manage accounting, tax, and payroll systems confidently.

How do I get started?

Just reach out via our contact form or WhatsApp. We’ll schedule a discovery call to understand your needs.

Is your service remote or in-person?

We offer both! Our team can work virtually or meet in person depending on your location and preference.

Do you offer monthly reporting?

Yes, we provide monthly financial statements and performance summaries to keep you informed.

Can you help with stocktaking?

We assist with accurate inventory, counts, audits and internal control systems to protect your assets.

What makes Accountpartners different?

We blend precision with personality—making financial clarity feel effortless and empowering.
